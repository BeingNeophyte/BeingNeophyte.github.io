Doctor Hero by JeTh Gaming

Doctor Hero is a game for windows platform in which the player has to deal with viruses and pills. This game consist of 8 different level, Avoid the viruses and collect all the pills to complete each level.

Genre

3D Adventure.

Platform

Windows.

Price

Free to download via itch.io

Age Range

Suitable for players of age 10 years and above

Plot / Story: A doctor goes on an adventure in search of a cure for a virus that is changing the whole world, fighting head on with viruses without letting himself be contaminated.

Gameplay: The game is structured in eight stages.
