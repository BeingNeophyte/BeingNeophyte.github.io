SunnyLand Forest Coppercube Source

SunnyLand Forest is a sidescroller 2D platformer game level demo created with coppercube. This a complete demo with Player,enemies,pickup items,jumps,ladder climbing,good animation blending, Camera following and many more. You can watch the video of the gameplay on your right to get the idea about the game. Demo also contains a starting and game over screen. This sourcefile contains a lot of javascript code, which is really easy to understand, it may look very complicated but it is really easy once you read out the commented codes.
